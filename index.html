LUNE

Elegant evening bag · Soft silhouettes

Materials

Supple Italian calfskin with a satin finish, lined with natural suede. Subtle gold-toned hardware, hand-polished. Magnetic closure concealed within the silhouette.

Statement

LUNE is an ode to nocturnal elegance. Softly sculpted and fluid, it moves effortlessly from dusk to evening, catching the light with quiet sophistication. Designed for moments that feel intimate yet unforgettable, LUNE embodies feminine grace, restraint, and timeless allure.

ULYSSE

Structured handbag · Heritage-inspired design

Materials

Full-grain Italian leather with a natural texture, reinforced leather panels, and hand-stitched edges. Interior lined in fine cotton twill. Palladium-finished hardware inspired by archival craftsmanship.

Statement

ULYSSE represents strength, journey, and legacy. With its architectural structure and refined detailing, it is a modern heirloom — grounded in tradition yet confidently contemporary. Designed for women who carry purpose with elegance, ULYSSE is a statement of enduring sophistication.

XÉLINE

Refined statement piece · Modern couture

Materials

Smooth French leather with a soft sheen, paired with sculptural hardware accents. Microfiber suede interior for a clean, modern finish. Precision-cut edges and seamless construction.

Statement

XÉLINE is bold refinement. Minimal yet expressive, it balances modern couture lines with effortless femininity. Created for those who command presence without excess, XÉLINE transforms simplicity into power, redefining luxury through clarity and confidence.

ORIANE

Luminous statement · Modern elegance

Materials

Fine-grain Italian leather with a soft sheen, complemented by sculptural metal accents. Interior lined in microfiber suede. Precision-finished edges for a clean, contemporary silhouette.

Statement

ORIANE captures light and movement. Radiant yet restrained, it reflects a modern vision of femininity — confident, fluid, and luminous. Created for moments that call for presence without ostentation, ORIANE is elegance in motion.

DIANE · PRINCESSE

Regal structure · Timeless authority

Materials

Structured full-grain leather with reinforced panels, hand-stitched detailing, and a natural leather lining. Palladium-finished hardware inspired by heritage equestrian codes.

Statement

DIANE · PRINCESSE embodies strength and nobility. With its architectural lines and composed silhouette, it reinterprets classic authority through a feminine lens. A modern heirloom, designed for women who lead with grace and conviction.

ÉLISE

Understated icon · Everyday refinement

Materials

Supple calf leather with a matte finish, paired with a soft cotton-sateen lining. Minimalist brushed metal hardware, crafted for durability and discretion.

Statement

ÉLISE is the essence of quiet luxury. Effortless, timeless, and impeccably balanced, it accompanies the everyday with natural elegance. Designed to transcend seasons, ÉLISE is refinement made instinctive.
